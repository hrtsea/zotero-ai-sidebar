手动 vs AI 写入,怎么选? Manual vs. AI-driven append — which one? 大段成型内容(如总结、对比表)用 AI 调 zotero_append_to_note 一次到位;边读边记的零碎想法手动复制更直接。两条路都不会丢消息 — 笔记面板和 AI 聊天是相互独立的工作区。 For chunky finished content (summaries, comparison tables), let the model call zotero_append_to_note in one shot. For ad-hoc reading notes, hover & copy is more direct. Either way: the note panel and chat are independent — neither overrides the other.
